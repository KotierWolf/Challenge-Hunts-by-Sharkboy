MISCELLANEOUS RULES

  • Before starting, enter the TRAINING AREA to REMOVE all beneficial buffs. FOOD or MEALS are fine, and you can choose which meal or food to eat.
  • ENVIROMENTAL DAMAGE is not allowed unless accidentally triggered via weapon attacks, or the monster self crashing themselves into them.
  • Place PALICOS on STANDBY
  • Items needed for CRAFTING more of the ALLOWED ITEMS are allowed to be in your LOADOUT, however they can not be used directly if they apply a beneficial buff. For example, IMMUNIZERS can be brought for crafting ANCIENT POTIONS, but can not be used for the natural healing buff.
